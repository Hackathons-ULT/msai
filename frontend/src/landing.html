HOW TO PLAY
ACTType what you want to do and press ACT. Suggestion chips below the input give you ideas - click one to fill it in.
DICERisky actions trigger a D20 roll. The agent best suited to the task rolls - high = success, mid = complications, low = failure.
PARTYYour AI teammates - Jax (Warrior), Lyra (Mage), Bram (Healer), Seren (Bard), and Kael (Smuggler) - each react and speak on their own.
POWEREach class has a one-per-session special ability. Hit the [!] POWER button during play. Hover it to see what it does.
XPEarn XP from dice successes and completed objectives. Hit 5 XP to level up. Each party member levels independently.
MAPClick MAP in the menu to see Aethelgard. Districts unlock as you travel - undiscovered areas stay dark until you visit them.
TRACEClick TRACE to watch the AI agents reason through your action in real time - plans, dice rolls, and state changes.
SAVEHit SAVE GAME to download your progress as a file. LOAD GAME restores it. Useful if you want to continue another day.
WORLDAethelgard - a steampunk city choked by the Clockwork Plague. Your mission: find out who caused it and stop it before it spreads.
