HOW TO PLAY
ACTType anything you want your character to do and press ACT. The AI will tell you what happens.
DICEWhen you do something risky, the game rolls a dice automatically. High roll = good outcome. Low roll = bad outcome.
PARTYYou have AI teammates (Warrior, Mage, Healer, Bard, Rogue) who each respond to your actions on their own.
TRACESee how the AI thinks. Click TRACE in the menu to watch the agents reason through your actions in real time.
RECAPClick RECAP to read the full story of your session from the beginning.
WORLDYou are in Aethelgard, a steampunk city being destroyed by a mysterious machine plague. Your mission: find out who caused it and stop it.
