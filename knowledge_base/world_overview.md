# World Overview: Aethelgard

## Setting the Tone
Aethelgard is a grim, highly industrialized metropolis where magic and machinery collide. The atmosphere is tense, oppressive, and shrouded in perpetual smog. The tone is low-fantasy steampunk: survival is hard, alliances are fragile, and every piece of technology looks improvised, heavily worn, or dangerously volatile.

---

## Geopolitical Divide

Aethelgard is vertically segregated. The wealth and safety of its citizens are entirely dependent on their altitude.

| District | Power Source | Atmosphere | Inhabitants |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Zenith Wards (Upper)** | Refined Crystal Magic | Pristine, sunlit, clean air | The Aristocrats, High Mages, Elite Guard |
| **The Sump (Lower)** | Sputtering Steam & Coal | Choking magical smog, dark | Laborers, criminals, outcasts, resistance fighters |
| **The Undercity (Ruins)** | Forgotten ancient tech | Toxic, lightless, collapsing | Forgotten horrors, plague mutants, lost explorers |

---

## The Imminent Threat: The Clockwork Plague

A supernatural disease is sweeping through The Sump. 
* **Transmission:** Spread through prolonged exposure to a newly mutated, dense pocket of magical smog.
* **Symptoms:** Victims lose their humanity, their veins glowing with a sickly copper light. Their movements become jerky and mechanical, earning them the moniker "Tick-Tocks."
* **Result:** They devolve into hostile, mindless husks driven only by the urge to spread the contagion or consume uncorrupted magic. 

---

## Absolute Laws of the Realm

Agents and players must navigate the world while adhering to—or carefully breaking—these established laws:
* **The Quarantine Edict:** The Zenith Wards have sealed the gates to The Sump; anyone attempting to climb the physical barriers is executed on sight by automated turrets.
* **Contraband Magic:** Unregistered magical artifacts or crystal-tech are highly illegal in The Sump and will be confiscated by corrupt Enforcers.
* **The Curfew of Ash:** When the city's central factory vents its exhaust at midnight, a thick, toxic ash falls over The Sump; anyone caught outside without a heavy respirator risks lung damage or plague exposure.
* **Guild Monopolies:** All healing supplies and mechanical parts are fiercely controlled by the Trade Guilds, making scavenging or black-market dealing the only ways to survive.

---

## The Ultimate Objective

The party is currently trapped in The Sump. Their only hope of saving the lower city—and themselves—is to descend into the treacherous Undercity to locate the mythic **Aether-Core**, a forgotten purification engine capable of clearing the plague-smog.
