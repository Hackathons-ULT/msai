# The Master Calibrator

## Description
A legendary device from before the surface died. A hand-sized brass cylinder with rotating dials and a glowing crystal core. It can manually override any pressure valve or steam regulator in Oakhaven.

## Current Status
Rumored to be hidden somewhere in the Pressure Core. The Engineers want it destroyed. The Sump-Workers want it to regulate the steam flow and save the lower decks.

## Significance
Whoever controls the Master Calibrator controls the pressure — and thus the future of Oakhaven.
