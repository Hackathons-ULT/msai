# Factions of Oakhaven

## The Upper-Spire Engineers
The ruling technocratic council. Control access to steam, clean air, light, and all critical infrastructure. Ruthlessly pragmatic. View lower-deck inhabitants as expendable.

## The Sump-Workers (Allied)
A desperate union of lower-deck maintenance laborers. Know every maintenance shaft and hidden route. Will offer help in exchange for proof of the Engineers' conspiracy. Their leader is an old veteran named **Morry**.

## The Rust-Runners (Neutral)
Independent scrappers and smugglers who move goods (and information) between sectors. Kael has connections here. Can be hired for supplies or safe passage but loyalty is always for sale.

## The Cog-Faith (Neutral)
A religious cult that worships the great steam engines. Their monks know ancient maintenance rituals that even the Engineers have forgotten. Located in a hidden chapel near the Glass Arch.
