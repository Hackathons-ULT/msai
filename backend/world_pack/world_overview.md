# World Overview: The Iron Labyrinth (Oakhaven)

## 1. The Setting & Geography
The world is **Oakhaven**, a massive, multi-layered industrial metropolis built entirely within a colossal subterranean cavern to escape the toxic atmosphere of a dead surface.

- **The Grid Sectors:** The city functions as a vast, interconnected network of sealed chambers. To travel between them, citizens use heavy pressure-sealed bulkheads and steam-powered rail tubes.
- **The Smog Layers:** The lower you go, the denser the soot and coal-smoke become. The lowest levels — The Undergrid — are completely dark, filled with toxic gas pockets and abandoned machinery.

## 2. Society & Rule
Oakhaven is ruled by the **Upper-Spire Engineers**, a technocratic council that controls access to steam, clean air, and light. The lower decks are populated by laborers, scrappers, and refugees.

## 3. Key Locations
- **Whispering Woods:** The upper-level entry point, a misty artificial forest with bioluminescent fungi.
- **The Iron Sluice:** A dimly lit drainage vault with leaking brass pipes and heavy static air.
- **The Sunken Market:** A flooded trading post halfway down, where information and contraband trade hands.
- **The Glass Arch:** An ancient reinforced transit hub connecting mid-level sectors.
- **The North Gate:** A primary security checkpoint to the deeper industrial sectors.
