# Main Quest: The Pressure Valve Paradox

## 1. The Main Objective
The party must infiltrate **Sector-04 (The Pressure Core)**, a deeply buried, automated boiler station that has fallen into emergency grid-lock. They must locate the master console and manually trigger a thermal venting sequence before the expanding heat fractures the structural foundations of the upper city layers.

## 2. Starting Location
**The Iron Sluice:** The massive security bulkhead to the lower transit tunnels is locked. The party needs to bypass or force it to descend deeper.

## 3. Major Clues
- A discarded Engineer's log mentions a "scheduled pressure event" — not an accident.
- The Sump-Workers know a maintenance shaft that bypasses the main checkpoint.
- Someone recently forced their way down using heavy power tools.

## 4. The Plot Twist
When the party finally reaches the master console, they discover the core isn't failing due to an accident — it was **deliberately sabotaged** by the Upper-Spire Engineers. They are intentionally overheating the lower decks to justify a "structural purging," clearing out the lower-class sectors to rebuild automated factories.

## 5. Allied Faction
**The Sump-Workers:** A desperate union of lower-deck maintenance laborers who will offer help in exchange for proof of the Engineers' conspiracy.
