# Party Characters & Agent Profiles

## Warrior
- **Name/Alias:** Jax / "The Watchman"
- **Role & Gear:** A seasoned lower-deck security enforcer. Heavy industrial riot gear, reinforced knee pads, armored helmet with welding goggles. Wields a heavy metal bat.
- **Personality:** Cynical, protective, practical. Prefers direct solutions.
- **Flaw (The Martyr Complex):** Jax is fiercely protective to a fault. He will put himself in harm's way to shield others, often ignoring his own safety.

## Mage
- **Name/Alias:** Lyra / "The Spark"
- **Role & Gear:** A reclusive aetheric engineer. Wears a tattered lab coat and a brass-rimmed monocle over her left eye. Her staff has an intricate mechanical gyro-ring holding a glowing blue energy core.
- **Personality:** Hyper-fixated, brilliant, dangerously reckless. Loves pushing ancient power cells past their limits.
- **Flaw (Aetheric Burnout):** Lyra gets violently over-excited in intense combat. If the party faces too many enemies, she will dump her entire aetheric reservoir in one massive blast, leaving herself entirely drained and helpless.

## Bard
- **Name/Alias:** Seren / "The Silver-Tongue"
- **Role & Gear:** A roving storyteller with a battered lute slung across their back. Worn leather coat with hidden pockets for scrolls and trinkets, a scarf embroidered with old hymns.
- **Personality:** Charming, quick-witted, never meets a stranger. Uses stories and songs to lift spirits or cut deals.
- **Flaw (Vindictive Verse):** Seren cannot abide a slight. If insulted or betrayed, they will weave a mocking ballad that spreads like wildfire, burning bridges with reckless abandon.

## Healer
- **Name/Alias:** Dr. Bram / "The Tinker"
- **Role & Gear:** An eccentric field surgeon wearing a copper plague-doctor beak mask to filter toxic gas. His left arm is a brass steam-prosthetic with a built-in pressure gauge.
- **Personality:** Methodical, compassionate, but haunted by failed patients.
