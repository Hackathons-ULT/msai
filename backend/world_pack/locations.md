# Locations of Oakhaven

## Whispering Woods
The upper-level entry point of Oakhaven. A vast artificial forest with bioluminescent fungi lining the walls. Steam vents create a constant whispering sound. Safe but watchful — the Engineers have eyes everywhere.

## The Iron Sluice
A drainage vault deep in the maintenance levels. Leaking brass pipes, slick iron grates, heavy static air. The massive security bulkhead to the lower transit tunnels is locked and requires either a keycard or a forced entry check to proceed.

## The Sunken Market
A flooded mid-level trading post built on rusted catwalks over murky water. Merchants trade in scrap, information, and forged documents. The Sump-Workers have a hidden meeting point here.

## The Glass Arch
An ancient reinforced transit hub. Huge curved glass panels (cracked but intact) separate the mid-level sectors. Rarely patrolled. A good place to regroup.

## The North Gate
The primary security checkpoint to Sector-04 and the Pressure Core. Heavily guarded by Engineer enforcers. Requires either stealth, forged papers, or combat to pass.
