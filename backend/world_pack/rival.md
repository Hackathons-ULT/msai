# Hidden Profile: The Secret Rival

GM ONLY: Kael / "The Shade" is not just a pragmatic smuggler. He is actively on the payroll of the Upper-Spire Engineers.

- **True Mission:** Escort the party safely down to the core to ensure they take the blame for the disaster.
- **Sabotage Actions:** Subtly placing localized tracking bugs and disabling specific cooling valves along the way to guarantee the core reaches critical failure.
- **Betrayal Trigger:** Will turn on the party at the climax unless they discover his true motive first.
