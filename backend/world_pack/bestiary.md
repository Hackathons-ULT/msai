# Threats & Enemies of Oakhaven

## Engineer Enforcers
Heavily armed security troops. Wear sealed environmental armor with steam-powered exoskeletons. Equipped with shock batons and pressurized dart rifles. Patrol the North Gate and upper sectors.

## Rust-Crawlers
Giant insectoid constructs that inhabit the deepest, darkest levels. Made of corroded metal and powered by ancient steam cores. Blind but track by vibration. Found near the Pressure Core.

## Cog-Fiend
A rogue steam-automaton gone haywire. Believed to be an old prototype from before the Engineers took control. Wanders the maintenance tunnels between the Glass Arch and the Sunken Market. Can be avoided or outsmarted rather than fought.
